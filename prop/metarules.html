Here are the rules!!

  • 1. Each player has to take turns.

  • 2. This game consists of two parts namely inner grid and an outer grid , where the outer grid consists of 9 inner grids where each consist of 3x3 grid tic tac toe.

  • 3. The game starts with player-1 where he can put his symbol in any of the 81 empty spots available.This move gives the opponent i.e., player-2 the chance to put his symbol in the relative position with respect to the outer grid.Then player-1 gets the chance to put a symbol in the relative position with respect to the outer grid.

  • 4. Once a player wins an inner grid ,that is frozen and no further moves can be made in it.

  • 5. The tic tac toe idea is applied to the inner grid as well as the outer grid.

  • 6. The game ends once a player wins the outer grid or there are no legal moves remaining in which case it is a draw.
